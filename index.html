What is it?

We are making NINE beacause we understand the excitement and fears behind planning to have a child. We want to help alleviate parents' fears of missing key steps along their pregnancy. At the same time, NINE helps you create a comprehensive set of memories from your pregnancy journey.

PERSONALIZE

The app recommends goals based on its understanding of your pregnancy.

VISUALIZE

See all your goals for a month in one place and fill them in with photo or video stories.

COMPILE

After your pregnancy, share a virtual baby book of your journey in having a kid.

PLAN

Keep yourself accountable for the goals you set for your pregnancy.

Our 3 Flows

Our design has gone through 3 iterations of user testing with mothers and fathers and it is being updated weekly.
1. Personalize the app to your family's stage in the pregnancy
2. Complete or add a pregnancy goal, capturing a photo memory along the way
3. Visualize your collection of memories in a photo book form

OUR PROCESS AND POINT OF VIEW

User Group

Parents planning out their pregnancy

Our Questions

HMW alleviate the fear new parents have of forgetting to hit all the important “steps” in having a baby?
HMW help parents capture the beautiful but often undocumented and grueling experience of the pregnancy, pre-birth?

Insight

There is a time of vulnerability in the pregnancy process that no other apps are catering to.
